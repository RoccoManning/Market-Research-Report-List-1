<p><p>A cancer vaccine is a type of biological therapy that aims to prevent or treat cancer by boosting the body's immune response against cancer cells. One of the prominent trends is the development of therapeutic cancer vaccines that aim to treat existing cancers. These vaccines are designed to target specific cancer antigens and stimulate the immune system to attack cancer cells. <p><p>The cancer vaccine market consists of two main types: preventive vaccines and therapeutic vaccines. On the other hand, therapeutic vaccines are intended for individuals already diagnosed with cancer and aim to stimulate the immune system to target and destroy cancer cells.
