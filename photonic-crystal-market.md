<p><p>The photonic crystal market encompasses three main types: one-dimensional, two-dimensional, and three-dimensional photonic crystals. One-dimensional photonic crystals consist of a periodic arrangement of layers with different refractive indices. Two-dimensional photonic crystals have a periodic arrangement of cylindrical or planar structures. Three-dimensional photonic crystals are made up of repetitive structures in all three dimensions. These crystals are used in various applications such as optical fibers, LEDs, solar panels, and sensors, providing enhanced control and manipulation of light for improved performance and efficiency.</p></p>
