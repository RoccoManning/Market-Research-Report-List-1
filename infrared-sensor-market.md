<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Infrared Sensor market comprises two major types: Thermopile Infrared Sensors and Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors. Thermopile Infrared Sensors work by detecting infrared radiation and converting it into electric voltage through the principle of a thermocouple. These sensors are known for their accuracy, high sensitivity, and wide detection range, making them suitable for applications such as temperature measurement and gas analysis. On the other hand, Pyroelectric Infrared Sensors generate an electric charge when exposed to rapid changes in temperature caused by infrared radiation. These sensors are commonly used in motion detectors, burglar alarms, and medical devices.</p></p>
