Firstly, the increasing adoption of cloud computing and the need for edge computing to support emerging technologies such as IoT and AI is driving the demand for these compact data centers. They allow organizations to process and analyze data closer to the source, reducing latency and improving performance.</p><p>Secondly, the rising demand for data center infrastructure in industries such as BFSI, healthcare, retail, and government is fueling market growth. These industries require on-site data processing capabilities to ensure data security, compliance, and real-time decision-making.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of Mobile/Micro Data Centers.
