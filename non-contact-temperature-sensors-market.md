The increasing adoption of automation and IoT technologies across industries is driving the demand for non-contact temperature sensors. As industries continue to integrate automation and digitization into their operations, the demand for accurate and reliable temperature sensing solutions like non-contact sensors is expected to grow.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing awareness about energy efficiency and environmental concerns is also contributing to the market's growth.
