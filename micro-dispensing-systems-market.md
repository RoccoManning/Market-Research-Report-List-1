<p><p>Micro dispensing systems are used in various industries for precise and accurate dispensing of small volumes of liquids or materials. The market can be categorized into two types: non-contact micro dispensing systems and contact micro dispensing systems. Non-contact micro dispensing systems utilize technologies like inkjet or aerosol dispensing, where the liquid is dispensed without directly contacting the substrate. On the other hand, contact micro dispensing systems involve direct physical contact between the dispensing nozzle and the substrate, such as needle dispensing or valve dispensing. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to specific application requirements.</p></p>
