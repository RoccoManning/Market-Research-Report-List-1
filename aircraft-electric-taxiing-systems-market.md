<p><p>Aircraft Electric Taxiing Systems (EGTS) can be categorized into two types: Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS and Vehicular Aircraft EGTS. Non-Vehicular Aircraft EGTS refers to systems that enable aircraft to taxi without the need for the main engines, using electric motors instead. These systems help in reducing fuel consumption and emissions. On the other hand, Vehicular Aircraft EGTS involves the use of electric vehicles or tugs to tow aircraft on the ground, providing a more efficient and eco-friendly alternative to traditional pushback tractors. Both types of EGTS contribute to the advancement of electric taxiing technology in the aviation industry.</p></p>
