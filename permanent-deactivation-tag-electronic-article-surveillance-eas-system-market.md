These tags can be permanently deactivated at the point of sale, eliminating the need for separate deactivation processes at store exits. This not only enhances the shopping experience for customers but also improves operational efficiency for retailers.</p><p>Moreover, the market is also being propelled by the growing adoption of RFID technology in EAS systems.
