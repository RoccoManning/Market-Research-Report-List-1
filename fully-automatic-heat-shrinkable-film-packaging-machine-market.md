<p><p>The fully automatic heat shrinkable film packaging machine market encompasses two types: V-FFS (Vertical Form Fill Seal) and H-FFS (Horizontal Form Fill Seal). V-FFS machines package products vertically, feeding the film downward and sealing it to form a bag. These machines are commonly used for small to medium-sized products such as snacks and candies. On the other hand, H-FFS machines package products horizontally, wrapping the film around the product and sealing it. These machines are typically used for larger products like boxes and cartons. Both types of machines offer automated packaging solutions and are widely used in various industries for efficient and effective product packaging.</p></p>
