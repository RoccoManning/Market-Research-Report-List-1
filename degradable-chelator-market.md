<p><p>A degradable chelator is a chemical compound that can bond with metal ions and is designed to break down into harmless substances over time. Degradable chelators offer the ability to bind and remove metal ions effectively while also being environmentally friendly. They are designed to break down into non-toxic by-products, reducing the risk of pollution and long-term environmental damage.</p><p>In terms of the current outlook, the degradable chelator market is poised for significant growth in the coming years.
