<p><p>Portable oil particle counters are used to measure and monitor the levels of contaminants and particles in oil. The market for these devices can be classified into three types: single channel particle counters, two channel particle counters, and multi-channel particle counters. </p><p>Single channel particle counters are capable of analyzing a single particle size range, providing information on the concentration of particles within that specific range. </p><p>Two channel particle counters can analyze two different particle size ranges simultaneously, allowing for more comprehensive monitoring of particle contamination.</p><p>Multi-channel particle counters are capable of analyzing multiple particle size ranges, providing the most detailed and accurate information about the distribution of particle sizes in the oil.</p></p>
