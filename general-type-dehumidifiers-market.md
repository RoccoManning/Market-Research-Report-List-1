<p><p>General type dehumidifiers are available in two different market types: mechanical type and electronic type. </p><p>Mechanical type dehumidifiers function by drawing air across a cooling coil to condense moisture, which is then collected and drained. They are usually more affordable and suitable for smaller spaces.</p><p>On the other hand, electronic type dehumidifiers employ advanced technology to remove moisture from the air. They use a fan to draw in air, which passes over a refrigerant-cooled coil to extract moisture. These dehumidifiers are often more effective and suitable for larger areas.</p></p>
