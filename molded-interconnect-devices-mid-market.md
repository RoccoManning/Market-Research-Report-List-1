<p><p>Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) are electronic components that combine mechanical structures and electrical circuits in one 3D molded part. There are various types of MID technologies available in the market. Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) involves using lasers to create conductive traces on a plastic component, enabling the integration of electrical circuits. Two-Shot Molding utilizes two different materials injected into the same mold to create a single component with both mechanical and electrical functions. Other market types include processes like three-dimensional circuit printing or metallization, offering alternative approaches for manufacturing MID components.</p></p>
