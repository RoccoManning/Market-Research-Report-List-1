<p><p>The Oily Wood Coating Market can be categorized into different types such as Solvent Borne, High-Solids, Radiation Curable, and Other market. Solvent Borne coatings are liquid coatings that contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) as solvents. High-Solids coatings have a higher percentage of solid content and lower VOC levels compared to solvent borne coatings. Radiation Curable coatings are cured using ultraviolet (UV) light or electron beam (EB) irradiation, providing instant drying and high durability. The Other market includes various types of wood coatings not falling under the aforementioned categories, such as water-based coatings, oil-based coatings, and powder coatings.</p></p>
