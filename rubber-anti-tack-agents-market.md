<p><p>Rubber anti-tack agents are substances used in the rubber industry to prevent the sticking or adhesion of rubber materials during storage or processing. There are different types of anti-tack agents available, including stearates, fatty acid esters, fatty acid amides, and soaps. Stearates are metal salts of stearic acid and offer good lubrication and release properties. Fatty acid esters are derived from fatty acids and provide excellent anti-tack properties. Fatty acid amides are amides of fatty acids and act as effective anti-stick agents. Soaps are formed by the reaction of fatty acids with alkalis and offer good anti-tack properties.</p></p>
