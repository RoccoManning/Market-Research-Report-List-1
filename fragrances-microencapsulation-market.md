<p><p>The Fragrances Microencapsulation Market Types include Physico-Mechanical Methods, Chemical Methods, and Physico-Chemical Methods. </p><p>Physico-Mechanical Methods involve techniques like spray drying and fluidized bed coating, where fragrance particles are physically coated with a protective substance. </p><p>Chemical Methods involve the use of chemical reactions to facilitate the encapsulation process, such as polymerization or interfacial polymerization. </p><p>Physico-Chemical Methods combine both physical and chemical techniques to encapsulate fragrances, such as coacervation or complex coacervation. These methods offer different approaches to effectively microencapsulate fragrances for various applications in industries like cosmetics, personal care, and household products.</p></p>
